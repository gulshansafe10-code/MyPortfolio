Project 02
Google Ads Lead Generation Campaign
Work Project  ·  Lumbini Elite Solutions, Bangalore  ·  2023–2024

The company had a Google Ads campaign that was running but not getting good results. I rebuilt it from scratch to get more leads at a lower cost.

The Problem
  • Ads were getting clicks but not converting into real leads
  • Cost per click was too high
  • Ad groups were messy and not well organized
What I Did
  • Reorganized all ad groups into tightly themed keyword groups
  • Built a negative keyword list to cut irrelevant traffic
  • Rewrote ad copy to focus on benefits, not just features
  • Switched to Max Conversions bidding after 30 days of data
  • Monitored performance daily and made small improvements regularly
Outcome

Click-through rate went up by 38%. Cost per click dropped by 22%. The number of leads grew while staying within budget. The campaign kept improving month over month.

What I Found
  • Google could not crawl some pages properly
  • Several broken links across the site
  • Missing or poorly written page titles and meta descriptions
  • No schema markup — so no rich results showing on Google
What I Did
  • Used SEMrush and Ahrefs to run a full site audit
  • Fixed all crawl errors and broken links
  • Rewrote titles and meta descriptions to match real search intent
  • Added schema markup for rich result eligibility
  • Tracked progress weekly using Google Search Console
